How to Use the Retirement Calculator

Step 1: Enter Your Information

Provide your monthly savings amount, the number of years until your retirement, and the annual rate of return you expect to earn on your savings.

Step 2: Calculate Retirement Savings

Click on the "Calculate Retirement Savings" button to see the estimated amount you'll have saved for retirement based on the provided information.

Step 3: Save Your Results

If you're satisfied with the calculated retirement savings, you can save the result for future reference by clicking the "Save This Result" button.

Step 4: View Saved Results

You can view your saved results in the "Saved Results" section below the calculator. You also have the option to delete any saved result if needed.

Step 5: Explore Other Features

Feel free to explore other features of the retirement calculator, such as getting a retirement quote or resetting the inputs.
